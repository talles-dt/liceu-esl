# Lexio Underground — Product Design (MVP)

## Vision

Lexio Underground (lexio) is an adaptive, AI-powered language learning service for Portuguese-native speakers, built on proven cognitive science principles: Krashen's comprehensible input, Sapir-Whorf structural awareness, memory palace mnemonics, and the three pillars of Grammar, Logic, and Communication.

## Core Loop (MVP)

1. User logs in (OAuth: Google/Apple or Magic Link)
2. Completes a **10-minute micro-lesson** (pillar-focused per day schedule)
3. Engages in a **5-minute chat with Leo**, the AI tutor (reinforcement + Q&A)
4. Receives next-day preview + streak confirmation

## Primary Goal

**Habit formation + mental structure mastery** — not just vocabulary accumulation.

## UX Principles (Zennial, PT-BR)

- Mobile-first, touch-centric interactions
- Snackable content (max 10 min/session)
- "Quirky" tone: whimsical, nonchalant, no corporate fluff
- Emoji-free interface (clean, professional)
- Dark mode default

## Monetization (TBD)

Leaning toward freemium/subscription. MVP will track usage to inform pricing tiers.

Potential model:
- **Free tier**: 5 lessons/week, Leo chat limited
- **Paid tier**: Unlimited lessons, full Leo access, tutor upsell path

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Why |
|--------|--------|-----|
| **North Star**: 30-day engagement rate | ≥60% | Validates habit formation |
| Supporting: Lesson completion rate | ≥85% | Content relevance |
| Supporting: Error recurrence rate | ↓ 20% MoM | Methodology efficacy |
| Supporting: Self-reported confidence | ↑ 1.5 pts (1-5 scale) | Affective filter reduction |

## Scope Guardrails (MVP)

- ✅ PT-BR → English only
- ✅ Text-based lessons + chat (no voice)
- ✅ 3-pillar rotation schedule
- ✅ Memory palace (text templates only)
- ✅ CEFR levels: A2, B1, B2, C1
- ❌ No social features
- ❌ No voice input/output
- ❌ No multi-language support
- ❌ No visual memory palace canvas (Phase 2)
- ❌ No leaderboards (Phase 2)
